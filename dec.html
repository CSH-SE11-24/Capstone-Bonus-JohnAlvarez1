What are Decepticon

The Decepticons are fictional sentient robots from the Transformers toy and entertainment franchise. Typically portrayed as the main antagonists opposing the Autobots, Decepticons are known for their insidious and deceitful nature, aiming to conquer and control the universe. Led by Megatron and distinguished by a purple, face-like insignia, these conscious robotic lifeforms possess the ability to transform into various alternate forms. These forms include high-tech vehicles like luxury and sports cars, military vehicles, aircraft, construction vehicles, and even extremely small objects.
